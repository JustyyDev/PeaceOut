Frequently Asked Questions

What is PeaceOut?
PeaceOut is a new kind of platform for video and live streaming. It aims for a peaceful, energetic, community-first vibe—think YouTube meets Twitch, but way more chill.
How do I upload a video or go live?
After creating an account and logging in, visit your dashboard to upload videos or get your stream key for OBS/live streaming.
Is PeaceOut free?
Yes! All core features are free. Some advanced features (like channel memberships or tips) may be added later as optional upgrades.
How do I report inappropriate content?
You can report videos or streams via the flag button below each video or by contacting us directly.
Can I customize my channel?
Absolutely! Update your profile info, avatar, and more from your profile settings. More customization features are coming soon.
How do I get notified when creators go live?
Follow your favorite creators and enable notifications in your account settings. We’ll keep you updated!
