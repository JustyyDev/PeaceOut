Frequently Asked Questions

What is PeaceOut?
PeaceOut is a next-generation platform for video and live streaming. It’s a peaceful, energetic, community-first home for creators and viewers—think YouTube meets Twitch, but with more chill vibes.
How do I upload a video or go live?
After creating an account and logging in, click Upload in the navigation to upload videos. To go live, visit your dashboard for your stream key and connect via OBS or any compatible software.
Is PeaceOut free?
Yes! All core features are free. Optional paid upgrades like channel memberships or tipping may be added in the future.
How do I report inappropriate content?
You can report videos or streams using the flag button or by contacting us directly.
Can I customize my channel?
Absolutely! Edit your profile info, avatar, and bio anytime. More channel customization features are coming soon.
How do I get notified when creators go live?
Follow your favorite creators and enable notifications in your account settings. We’ll let you know whenever they go live or upload new content!
