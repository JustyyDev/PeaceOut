Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last updated: August 24, 2025
PeaceOut is a community-first video and livestream platform. Your trust, safety, and privacy are our priority.
By using PeaceOut, you agree to these terms and our privacy practices.

1. What We Collect

  • Account Information: username, email, password (hashed), avatar, and optional bio.
  • User Content: videos, livestreams, comments, chat messages, reactions.
  • Usage Data: logins, page views, device/browser info, IP address, preferences, and activity logs.
  • Cookies & Tracking: session cookies for login, analytics (anonymized, non-invasive).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the PeaceOut platform.
  • To personalize your experience and recommend content.
  • To enable social features (chat, comments, likes, notifications).
  • To ensure community safety and enforce our guidelines.
  • To send you platform updates, security alerts, or new feature announcements (only if you opt in).
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse or fraud.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties.
  • We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., video hosting, analytics) only as needed to run PeaceOut.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect PeaceOut, its users, or the public.
  • Public content (videos, comments, live chat) is visible to all users and may appear in search or recommendations.

4. Your Rights & Choices

  • You can access, update, or delete your account at any time from your profile settings.
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • You may opt in or out of email updates and notifications.
  • For EU/EEA users: you have rights under GDPR (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, data portability).

5. Data Security & Retention

  • Passwords are always securely hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Uploads are stored securely; only you and the community can access your public uploads.
  • We retain your data only as long as needed to operate PeaceOut and comply with laws.
  • If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data except where retention is required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

  • PeaceOut is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
  • If you believe we have collected such data, contact us for removal.

7. Platform Rules & Acceptable Use

  • No illegal, hateful, or harmful content. Respect others. Follow local and international laws.
  • No spamming, harassment, or abusive behavior.
  • No copyright infringement. Upload only content you own or have rights to share.
  • No attempts to access or disrupt others’ accounts, data, or the platform itself.
  • We may remove content or suspend accounts that violate these rules, per our discretion.

8. Updates to This Policy

  • We may update these terms and our privacy policy as PeaceOut evolves. We’ll notify you of major changes.
  • Continued use of PeaceOut after updates means you accept the new terms.
